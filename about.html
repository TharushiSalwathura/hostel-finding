About Us

StayNest is your trusted platform for finding safe, affordable, and convenient hostels. Whether you’re a student, traveler, or working professional, we help you discover the perfect place that fits your lifestyle and budget.

Our Mission

At StayNest, our mission is simple:

  • To make hostel hunting easier and faster.
  • To connect people with verified, comfortable, and budget-friendly stays.
  • To create a transparent platform where both hostel owners and seekers benefit.

Terms & Conditions

By using StayNest, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate information when creating an account or listing.
  • Use the platform only for legal and genuine hostel-related purposes.
  • Respect hostel rules and policies provided by each property.
  • Understand that StayNest acts as a connecting platform and does not own or operate listed hostels.

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and ensure:

  • Your personal data is securely stored and never sold to third parties.
  • Information shared will only be used to improve services and provide better recommendations.
  • You have control over your account and data, with the option to update or delete anytime.
